How to Play

Use arrow keys or (wasd) to move left and right. Press space/up arrow/w to jump. Enemies can be killed if stepped on or by using a weapon. Press f or control to fire (only the snowperson character has a weapon). Score can be increased by killing enemies or by collecting carrots (hidden inside boxes).
